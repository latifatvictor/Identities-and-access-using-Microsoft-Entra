# 👨‍💼 Day in the Life of an IAM Engineer (Microsoft Entra)

## 📌 Scenario
You are an Identity & Access Administrator managing a new employee lifecycle.

---

## 🟢 08:30 – New Employee (Joiner)

Step 1 > Create new user in Entra ID  
Step 2 > Assign license (M365)  
Step 3 > Add to department group  
Step 4 > Assign role (if needed)  
Step 5 > Enforce MFA  

✅ Outcome:
User can securely access company resources

---

## 🟡 11:00 – Role Change (Mover)

Step 1 > Update user department  
Step 2 > Remove old group memberships  
Step 3 > Add new group memberships  
Step 4 > Update role assignments  

✅ Outcome:
User has correct access for new role

---

## 🔴 15:00 – Employee Leaves (Leaver)

Step 1 > Disable user account  
Step 2 > Revoke active sessions  
Step 3 > Remove licenses  
Step 4 > Remove group memberships  
Step 5 > Archive data  

✅ Outcome:
Access fully revoked

---

## 🔐 16:00 – Security Hardening

Step 1 > Review Conditional Access policies  
Step 2 > Enable MFA enforcement  
Step 3 > Check risky sign-ins  
Step 4 > Review admin roles  

✅ Outcome:
Environment secured against threats

---

## 📊 17:00 – Maintenance & Monitoring

Step 1 > Review audit logs  
Step 2 > Perform access reviews  
Step 3 > Check inactive accounts  
Step 4 > Clean old groups  

✅ Outcome:
Environment stays secure and optimized
